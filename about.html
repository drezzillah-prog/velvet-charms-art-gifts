About Velvet Charms — Art & Gifts

Velvet Charms is a small studio focused on handcrafted artwork and giftable home pieces. We specialize in mixed-media paintings and sculptural epoxy/clay decorations that make unique keepsakes. Top-selling categories include paintings, epoxy & clay creations, leather goods and artisan wall clocks.

Commissions & Customization

We love custom work: portraits, custom landscape commissions and festive portraits are available. When you order a commission you can choose materials, colors and send reference images. Use the product customization box on any product page to detail what you want — we’ll confirm a mockup before starting the final piece.
