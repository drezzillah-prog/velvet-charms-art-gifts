About Velvet Charms — Art & Gifts

Velvet Charms is a small, family-run studio crafting unique handmade gifts and home pieces with an emphasis on personalization and festive spirit. Every item you see — from hand-poured epoxy pendants to whimsical clay figurines and cozy winter bundles — is created with care and can be customized for your loved ones.

This season we’re focused on creating heartfelt Christmas presents: custom portraits, personalized phone cases, and custom-made gift boxes ready to surprise under the tree. Tell us the colors, initials, or motifs you love and we’ll design a one-of-a-kind piece. We offer bundled discounts for gift sets, fast custom-commission turnarounds, and secure PayPal checkout links on every product page.

Shopping here supports a small maker who pours time and love into each piece — perfect for meaningful holiday gifts that will be treasured for years. If you want help choosing or customizing, contact us and we’ll help you craft the perfect present.
